About Us

Our story

In the heart of our community beats the spirit of compassion and empowerment we are more than a charity organization we are a beacon of hope , dedicated to transforming lives and uplifting the most vulnerable amongst us

Our inception has been steadfast advocate for those facing adversity , working tirelessly to alleviate suffering and restore dignity with a deep rooted commitment to compassion and service , we have touched countless lives offering a lifeline to those in need

“We believe every individual deserves a chance to thrive regardless of their circumstances. We stand alongside the marginalized, the oppressed, the forgotten offering a helping hand and glimmer of hope"

The people behind SHENDI

Our Team

Four women leading SHENDI's mission across Mbale City and Sironko district.

CEO

Ms. WETSETSE SUZAN

The CEO, is a graduate of Ethics and Human Rights who with the hope to reach out to girls, speak and help find them ways to tackle daily life challenges, founded SHENDI FOUNDATION. Her musical career thus plays a pivotal role in reaching out to the masses thereby breaking barriers within the youth

Board Member

Ms. HARRIET KAKAI

serves as the Mbale City Deputy Mayor, a position she has held for 8 years right from municipality to city status.

Secretary

Ms. WETSETSE ESTHER LOIS

Serves as the manager at Elgon Dinery, and holds a bachlors degree of arts in education

Board Member

KAKAYI SHAKIRA

Serves as the Deputy speaker Sironko district, a position she has held since 2021 and she is the Secretary at the office of the RDC Mbale since 2023.
